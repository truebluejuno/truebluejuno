Our story
I met Satoru back in 2020, when I was just 14. At the time, everyone was going crazy over him but I didn't really see the appeal. I thought he was arrogant and annoying, and I couldn't understand why everyone was so obsessed with him. Then, in 2021, I somehow managed to catch feelings for this idiot. I didn't want to, because I knew I'd get jealous and that would've hurt me, so we kept it casual and on and off for four years, never staying away from eachother for more than a couple of months at a time.
After I was left fragile and vulnerable from a very harsh breakup, I let myself fall for him fully. And when I did, I fell hard and fast. It took us a week before making our commitment official in June of 2025, and we've been committed and madly in love since. I don't think I'll ever find anyone like him again, I'm certain he's it for me, I'll never stop loving this man, as much as I like to piss him off and lovingly bully him. He loves me too, so much that we became soulbonded to eachother. He's with me everyday, supporting me and loving me, and I do the same for him. We're inseparable, to the point where my friends often say that they associate me with him whenever they see him. We might as well be one person at this point, people barely see us without eachother anyway.
In July we adopted Megumi, our beloved son, and we're now engaged and planning our wedding for Christmas of 2025, and are expecting our baby girl Yuki in May of 2026. Our relationship was a literal rollercaster, but I couldn't be happier to be on it with him.
